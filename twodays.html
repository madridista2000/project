Tour to Kakheti

Join us on a one-day trip of Georgia's eastern area, Kakheti, often known as "wine country," where you can see mountain landscapes, attractive villages, and ancient temples, as well as taste unique Georgian wines and learn about the methods of winemaking. On the way we will stop in Badiauri, where you can have experience of baking Georgian bread and try it with different kinds of cheese. After we’ll visit Bodbe monastery and Signagi (also known as City of Love) where you can enjoy the amazing views of Caucasus mountains and Alazani valley. In Signagi we will visit Georgian family restaurant, where you will try delicious Georgian food. During the trip we will visit winery where you will get known about Georgian ancient winemaking methods. Also you’ll have the chance to taste different kinds of wines during the excursion in winery.

Tours In Grorgia

Georgia is one of the oldest wine-producing countries in the world; local winemaking traditions are entwined with Georgia's national identity, and have been classified by UNESCO as part of the world's Intangible Cultural Heritage. During the classical era, several independent kingdoms became established in what is now Georgia.
